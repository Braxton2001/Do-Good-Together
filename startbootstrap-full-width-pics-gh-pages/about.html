People management and development

Doing Good, Together, For Together We Are Better. My purpose is to cultivate growth—in myself and others—by fostering meaningful connections, embracing creativity, and living in alignment with my values. Through my career in Human Resources, I aim to create environments where people feel empowered, supported, and inspired to grow. I strive to approach life with a sense of wonder, appreciating the beauty in nature and the world around me. Grounded in my faith I seek to live with kindness, wisdom, power and to do good, together. I am always excited to meet new people! Connect with me!

Goals and Aspirations

Where I see myself in 5-10 years

My expected graduation date from Brigham Young University is April 2026. I plan on getting SHRM certified in my last year of university, helping me in my career as a HR professional. After graduating I am considering an International MBA in Barcelona, Spain. I have always loved different cultures and languages and the social blend of our collective humanity. I speak Spanish and I interned in Spain in Valencia in 2024, and I loved it. Throughout all generations, nations, and languages, we are all human, and I believe humans are inherently good. We are one body, one voice, and one people, and there is power in that understanding. I want to utilize that power to make the world a better and more connected place.

After obtaining an International MBA, I plan to either work in the US or in Europe. I want to work for creative companies. Life is fun and full of unknown territory, and untold stories. I want to help employees tell these stories and help them create content that brings imagination to life and make dreams a reality.

My ultimate goal is to eventually become a CHRO of a company. I am passionate about HR and its impact on a company and the good HR brings to the table. Organizations are better places to work and make a larger impact on the world when HR departments are empowered to implement strategic practices. We are the guardians of organizations and society is comprised of systems and organizations; we have a sacred duty to influence organizations and therefore society for good.
